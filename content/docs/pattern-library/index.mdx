### Full-page layouts

While the pattern library is mostly page sections, we’ve built full-page layout patterns that can be used as starters when adding a new page or post in WordPress.

## Core Kindling patterns

The Kindling WordPress theme ships with a curated list of patterns:

- **Home Page Hero** – A high-impact section at the top of the home page featuring a looping video, bold headline, supporting text, and a CTA to drive engagement.
- **Visual Hero** (*Page Header – Hero*) – A large, visually driven header section with an image or background graphic.
- **Basic Hero** (*Page Header – Simple*) – A simple, text-based header with a button.
- **Content Centered** (*Section header*) – A centered text block, often used for introductions or key messaging areas where a single idea is highlighted.
- **Split Content with image** (*formerly 50/50*) – A balanced layout with text on one side and media on the other.
- **Split content** – A balanced layout with multiple columns of text.
- **Card** – Clickable content blocks used to link out to other landing pages.
- **Featured Content** – A visual section used to highlight a large group of pages (technologies, industries, etc.) and provide links to these pages.
- **Icon Grid** – A structured layout featuring icons with headlines and short descriptions.
- **Testimonial/Case Study Slider** (*Testimonial/Case Study*) – A rotating slider showcasing customer testimonials or case studies which can display a video or a static image.
- **Client Logo Bar** (*Logo Cloud*) – A section displaying client logos to build trust and demonstrate industry expertise.
- **Featured Articles** – A section highlighting recent or relevant articles.
- **Stats** – A visually engaging section displaying key statistics or metrics.
- **FAQs** – A structured, collapsible section for frequently asked questions.
- **CTA** – Strategically placed buttons or forms encouraging users to take the next step, such as contacting StreamTech or submitting a form.
