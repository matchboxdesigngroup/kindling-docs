## What Is Kindling Blocks?

Kindling Blocks is our custom-built library of high-quality WordPress blocks designed to give you more flexibility, speed, and consistency when creating new pages on your website. It extends the WordPress block editor with enhanced layout tools, interactive components, and reusable patterns that match your brand and design system.

With Kindling Blocks, you can build pages that look professional, load quickly, and remain easy to maintain without relying on heavy page builders. Each block is optimized for performance, accessibility, and long-term stability, ensuring your site stays fast and future-proof as content evolves.
